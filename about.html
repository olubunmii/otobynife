About Ọtọ̀

Rooted in the soil. Grown with purpose.

Our Story

Ọtọ̀ — meaning "different" or "seperate" in Yoruba — was born from a simple belief: that everyone deserves access to food that is not processed, fresh, honest, and grown with care.


Founded by Nife, the farm started as a small greenhouse operation and grew into a full-scale agricultural business supplying fresh produce, dairy, meat, grains, and more to homes and businesses.


We work closely with local farmers, keeping our supply chain short so what reaches you is as fresh as the morning it was harvested.

What We Stand For

🌿
Freshness First

We harvest on demand and deliver quickly. No cold storage for weeks, only real and fresh food.

🤲
Community Roots

We support local farmers by giving them a platform to sell and grow. When they thrive, we all do.

♻️
Sustainable Farming

We practice responsible farming — minimal chemicals, healthy soil, and respect for the land.
